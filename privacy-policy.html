Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Calory application ("Calory", "the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. Calory is an AI nutrition app that estimates the calories and nutrients in your meals from a photo. We built Calory to keep your personal data on your own device wherever possible. There are no user accounts, no advertising, and we do not sell your data.

In short: Your food log, weights and profile stay on your device and sync only to your own private iCloud. When you scan a meal or chat with the AI coach, that specific photo or message is sent securely to a third-party AI provider for real-time analysis and is not kept afterwards. We use no advertising and no cross-app tracking.

1. Information We Process

Data that stays on your device. The information you enter and generate in the app — your name or nickname, sex, date of birth, height, weight entries, goals, activity level, logged meals, water intake, streaks and your coach chat history — is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, this data also syncs to your own private iCloud account through Apple's CloudKit so it is available on your other devices. We cannot access the contents of your private iCloud storage.

Data sent for AI analysis. When you take or select a photo of a meal, that image is sent over an encrypted connection to our server and then to a third-party AI provider to identify the foods and estimate nutrition. When you use the AI coach or AI recipe features, the text you send together with limited context (such as your goal and your daily macro totals) is sent the same way. This content is processed to generate a response and is not stored by us after the response is returned.

Data used to run the service. To provide free-tier limits and prevent abuse, the app generates a random, anonymous device identifier that is sent with each request and used to count usage. If you subscribe to Calory Pro, a purchase transaction identifier is sent to our server so it can verify your subscription with Apple. We may also process basic technical information such as your IP address and app version as an ordinary part of handling network requests.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require an account, email address, phone number or password.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers and do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.
  • We do not access the nutrition, weight or profile data held in your private iCloud.

3. How We Use Information

  • To analyze your meal photos and return calorie and nutrient estimates.
  • To generate coaching answers and recipes from the AI features.
  • To apply free-tier limits and to verify Calory Pro subscriptions.
  • To maintain the security, reliability and basic operation of the service.

We rely on the necessity of providing the service you request, and your consent (given by choosing to scan a meal or use the AI features), as the legal bases for this processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted providers to run Calory. They process data only to perform their function for us and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes:

  • A third-party AI provider — provides the artificial-intelligence models that analyze meal photos and generate coach and recipe responses.
  • Cloudflare — hosts the server that receives requests from the app and forwards them for analysis.
  • Apple — provides iCloud storage/sync for your on-device data and processes subscription payments through the App Store. Payments are handled entirely by Apple; we never see your card details.

5. Artificial Intelligence

Calory uses AI to estimate nutrition and to generate coaching and recipes. AI output is an estimate and may be inaccurate. It is provided for general informational purposes only and is not medical, dietary, or health advice. Please review and adjust the results, and consult a qualified professional for any decision that affects your health.

6. International Data Transfers

Because our providers operate globally, your information may be processed on servers located outside your country, including in the United States. Where required, these transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Data Retention

  • On-device and iCloud data is kept until you delete it. You can erase everything from within the app using "Delete all data & reset", which also removes the copy in your iCloud.
  • Meal photos and AI messages are processed in real time and are not retained after the response is generated.
  • Anonymous usage counters tied to the random device identifier are kept only as long as needed to enforce daily limits and are automatically cleared on a short cycle.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and to data portability. Because most of your data lives on your device, you can exercise many of these rights directly: view and edit your information in the app, or remove it entirely with "Delete all data & reset". For any other request, contact us at the address below and we will respond as required by law.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request its deletion, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

10. Children

Calory is not intended for children under 16 years of age (or a higher age where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Security

Data transmitted to and from the app is encrypted in transit. On-device data is protected by your device's own security. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users and the relevant authorities where required by applicable law and within the timeframes it sets.

13. Cookies and Tracking

Calory does not use advertising cookies or cross-app tracking technologies, and does not build advertising profiles about you.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page, and where appropriate, notified in the app.

15. Your Consent

By using Calory, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the processing described in it.

16. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact us at calorysnap@gmail.com.

Frequently asked

How does Calory work?

Take a photo of your meal and Calory's AI recognizes the foods, estimates portions, and gives you calories, protein, carbs, fat and micronutrients. You can adjust the grams or items before saving.

Is my data private?

Your food log, weights and profile stay on your device and sync privately through your own iCloud. Meal photos and coach messages are sent securely to our server for AI analysis and are not stored afterwards. There are no accounts and no advertising.

What does Calory Pro include?

Pro unlocks unlimited meal scans, the AI coach, AI recipes and the full micronutrient breakdown. New subscribers start with a 3-day free trial.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions, choose Calory and tap Cancel Subscription. If you cancel during the free trial you won't be charged.

I paid but Pro isn't active

Open Calory, go to the Pro screen and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe. If it still doesn't work, email us.

The AI got my meal wrong

AI estimates aren't perfect. On the results screen you can rename items, change the grams, add or remove foods before you save — your correction is what gets logged.
